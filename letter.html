Dear Salmon,

Today must have been my blessed day, i don’t know if you think so too?
Maybe i should thank the universe from bringing you to this world and from bringing you to me ,
i think this may have been the most beautiful gift the world had granted me, but idk.

I know that we’re not in love or anything, and it doesn’t matter to me,
to be honest it doesn’t really matter, because
i think being able to meet you is enough blessing for me already,
and i didn’t want to ask too much.

I don’t know if i want you to be happy with other person,
but i do hope that you’ll always be happy no matter what.
And you’ll always be surrounded with people who loves you.

I’m sincerely thanking you for all of the time we spent together.
For you’ve always supported me and believe in me when i don’t even believe in myself,
though i don’t know if it was sincere, i do feel most grateful for it.
And you’ve always convinced me to do more even when i don’t believe i can do it.

I can’t believe it when you said i’ve grown mature enough, because i’m not.
I know i’m just a messy confused child who need guidance from someone.
And honestly all those time i’ve spent being busy was just to make you proud.

But really, i’m so glad that we’ve ever known each other at all.
And for all that you’ve giving me, I sincerely said thank you and sorry.
And this is the least i can do to make you happy.

Happy birthday,
and may you live long enough to celebrate many other day in your live.
